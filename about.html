About

Hello, I’m Anne!

I love taking photos—simply documenting what I see: colors, moments, shapes, and the compositions life gives us. I see them with my heart first, then with my eyes.

Kids are always around me, and I’ve been documenting my children’s universe for the last ten years. It’s chaos and imperfection, yet full of beauty if you just let them be.

I value film photography for its honesty and its stunning colors. Because film isn’t always affordable, I embrace digital as well—both have their own magic.

I also make sustainable frames. For any inquiries, just shoot me a message.

If you’d like me to document a little snippet of your life, get in touch — I’d love to hear from you.
